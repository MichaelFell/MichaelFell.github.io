InspireXR

Project Description

I was tasked with creating the home scene of Reality Garage's Virtual Reality platform, InspireXR. InspireXR is a tool for creating and sharing user-made VR experiences, and this scene serves as a jumping off point for browsing, downloading, and launching content.

We wanted to incorporate the natural beauty of Boulder Colorado while showcasing the blend of CG objects and 360 pictures/videos possible in the platform, so I built this scene atop the Sanitas trail, just a hike away from the office.

Tools Used

  • Unity
  • Blender
  • Photoshop
